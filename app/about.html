About Analyzer

Welcome to Resume Analyzer, a sophisticated tool designed to help you rank resumes based on their relevance to a given job description. By leveraging advanced techniques such as cosine similarity and TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency), our tool provides an accurate and efficient way to evaluate and compare resumes.

Simply upload the resumes and provide the job description, and our system will analyze the documents to rank them based on their similarity to the job requirements. This makes the hiring process more streamlined and helps you find the most suitable candidates quickly.
